About exploro.com TM

Founded in 2019 in Kathmandu, Booking.com has grown from start-up to one of the Nepal’s leading digital hotel room booking companies. Part of Booking Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: BKNG), exploro.com’s mission is to make it easier for everyone to experience the online booking system throughout the Nepal.

By investing in technology that takes the friction out of travel, exploro.com seamlessly connects thousands of visiters to memorable experiences, a variety of accommodation options, and incredible places to stay – from high himalayas to the lower valley in Terai region and much more. As one of the Nepal’s largest travel marketplaces for both established brands and entrepreneurs of all sizes, exploro.com enables properties around the country to reach a mass audience and grow their businesses.
